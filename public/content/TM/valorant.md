
# Valorant Tournament

**Aim Sharp. Play Smart. Dominate the Arena.**

Get ready to enter the battlefield and showcase your **tactical brilliance** in the **Valorant Tournament**.

This high-intensity esports competition is designed for players who thrive under pressure and excel in **teamwork, strategy, and precision**. Whether you're a skilled **duelist**, a strategic **controller**, or a supportive **initiator**, this is your chance to prove your dominance in the arena.

Gear up, coordinate with your team, and fight your way to **victory**!

---

## 📍 Event Overview

* Participation is open to **teams of 5 players**
* Each team may register with **1 substitute player (optional)**
* All matches will follow a **structured knockout format**
* Matches will be played in a **supervised and fair-play environment**

---

## 🔹 Tournament Format

### Preliminary Rounds

* Matches will be played in a **knockout format**
* Each match will follow the **standard competitive rules**
* The **winning team advances** to the next stage

---

### Semi-Finals

* Qualified teams will compete in **higher-intensity matches**
* **Strategy, communication, and coordination** will play a crucial role

---

### Grand Finale

* The **top two teams** will battle for the championship title
* The team that secures the **final victory** will be crowned champions

---

## 📜 Rules & Guidelines

* All players must follow **official in-game competitive rules**
* Any use of **unfair practices or exploits** will result in disqualification
* Teams must **report at least 30 minutes before match time**
* Decisions made by the **event coordinators will be final**

---

## 🧑‍⚖️ Winning Criteria

* Match victories
* Team coordination and strategy
* Sportsmanship and fair play
