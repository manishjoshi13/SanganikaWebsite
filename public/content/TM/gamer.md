# Gamer’s Roulette

**Spin the Wheel. Test Your Skill. Every Game, A New Thrill!**

Welcome to **Gamer’s Roulette**, an electrifying gaming event where **luck meets skill** and every spin brings a new challenge.

From action-packed battles to brain-teasing puzzles, the **roulette wheel decides which game you play next**. Adapt quickly, play smart, and survive each round to stay in the competition.

Every spin could change your fate — **are you ready?**

---

## 📍 Event Overview

* **Solo participation only**
* Participants must bring a **mobile phone capable of supporting basic games**
* The event consists of an **elimination round followed by roulette-based knockout rounds**

---

## 🔹 Round Structure

### Round 1: Elimination Round

* This is the **initial screening round**
* Participants will compete in a **designated game**
* Game details will be shared **before the event begins**
* Based on performance, selected participants will move forward to the **roulette rounds**

---

### 🎡 Roulette Rounds

An exciting **spinning wheel** determines which game will be played in each round.
The games will be **fun, easy to understand, and from different genres** to ensure variety and excitement.

---

### Part One: Head-to-Head Battles

* The **wheel is spun** to select a game
* **Two participants compete at a time**
* **Winners advance** to the next stage
* **Losers are eliminated**
* This continues until **all participants have competed**

---

### Part Two: Knockout Progression

* Remaining participants continue **spinning the wheel and competing**
* **Each round eliminates players**
* The process continues until **only two finalists remain**
* The final match determines the **Winner and Runner-Up**

If there is an **odd number of participants**, a special elimination round will be conducted based on a predefined parameter.
