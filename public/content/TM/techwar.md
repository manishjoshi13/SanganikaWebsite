# Tech Wars

**Strategize. Innovate. Conquer.**

### Dominate the Battlefield of Tech Mastery!

**Tech Wars** is the ultimate **tech quiz and visualization competition** designed to test your knowledge, analytical thinking, and awareness of the ever-evolving technology world.

This dynamic event challenges participants with **exciting tech-based questions, interactive rounds, and a final judgment showdown** where your insights and decision-making skills take center stage.

Compete, think fast, and prove that you are truly **tech-savvy**.

---

## 📍 Event Overview

* Participants may register **individually or as a duo**
* The competition consists of **three rounds**
* The first two rounds focus on **technical knowledge and aptitude**
* The final round is a **judgment-based challenge** that tests your understanding of **future technology trends**

---

## 🔹 Round Structure

### Round 1: Tech & Aptitude Challenge

* Participants will answer a variety of:

  * **Aptitude-based questions**
  * **Technology-related questions**
* This round tests **logical reasoning and core technical awareness**

---

### Round 2: Tech Literature & Visualization

This interactive round includes two segments:

#### Tech Visualization

Participants will identify:

* **Company logos**
* **CEOs**
* **Technologies used in movies and TV shows**

Hints will be provided during the round.

#### Tech Literature

Questions will be based on:

* **Famous tech quotes**
* **Technology-related acronyms**

Accuracy and speed will be **key to advancing**.

---

### Round 3: Tech Sharks (Final Round)

In the final showdown, participants will:

* Make **informed predictions about the future of technology**
* Provide **logical reasoning** behind their answers
* Demonstrate awareness of **current tech trends**

Participants will be evaluated based on:

* Technical knowledge
* Clarity of thought
* Awareness of modern tech developments
