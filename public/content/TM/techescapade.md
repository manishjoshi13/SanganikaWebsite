
# Tech Escapade

**Decode. Discover. Escape the Ordinary.**

Step into a world where **technology meets adventure**.

**Tech Escapade** is an immersive, challenge-based competition designed to test your **technical knowledge, logical reasoning, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities**. Participants will navigate through a series of **tech-driven puzzles, clues, and tasks**, each bringing them closer to the final breakthrough.

This isn’t just a competition. It’s a **journey through innovation, strategy, and quick thinking**.

---

## 📍 Event Overview

* Participants can register **individually or in teams of 2–3 members**
* The event will consist of **multiple progressive levels**
* Each level must be cleared to **unlock the next challenge**
* The competition is **time-bound and highly interactive**

---

## 🔹 Event Structure

### Level 1: The Initiation

* Participants will solve **basic technical and logical puzzles**
* Questions may include **coding concepts, aptitude, or tech trivia**
* Successful completion **unlocks the next level**

---

### Level 2: The Code Trail

Participants will encounter:

* **Debugging challenges**
* **Pattern recognition tasks**
* **Short coding problems**

Clues will guide participants **step-by-step toward the final challenge**.

---

### Level 3: The Great Escape

The final level will test:

* **Analytical thinking**
* **Decision-making skills**
* **Technical awareness**

Participants must solve the **final set of interconnected problems** to successfully complete the escapade.

---

## 🧑‍⚖️ Winning Criteria

* Accuracy of solutions
* Speed of completion
* Logical approach
* Team coordination

The **team or individual who completes all levels in the shortest time with maximum accuracy** will be declared the winner.
