
# Bumble Jumble

**Byte by Byte — Turning Chaos into Code!**

Step into a world where nothing makes sense… until you solve it.

**Bumble Jumble** is an exciting **puzzle-based web development event** where logic meets creativity. Participants will tackle a series of **jumbled code snippets, scrambled layouts, and tricky questions** that challenge their understanding of front-end development fundamentals.

This event is designed to test your **quick thinking, accuracy, and problem-solving skills** — all while making code-cracking a fun and competitive adventure.

---

## 📍 Event Overview

* Participants may register **individually or in teams of two**
* Questions will be based on **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**
* The competition will be conducted in **two rounds**

---

## 🔹 Round 1: The Puzzle Sprint (30 Minutes)

* Teams will be given **30–40 questions**

* The questions will include:

  * Jumbled **HTML/CSS/JS snippets**
  * **Fill-in-the-blank** code
  * **Output / behavior-based** questions
  * **Multiple-choice questions**

* All questions will focus on **fundamental concepts of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**

* The **top 10 teams** with the highest number of correct answers within the time limit will qualify for the **final round**

---

## 🔹 Round 2: The Final Unjumble

The **top 10 selected teams** will face **4 challenging questions**:

* Two **web development tasks** containing:

  * errors
  * jumbled sections
  * missing parts

* Two **JavaScript logic/debugging problems**

Participants must **rearrange, debug, and complete the code correctly**.

The team that completes **all questions accurately in the shortest time** will be declared the winner.
