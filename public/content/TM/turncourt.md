# Turn Court

**Where Debates Shift and Sharp Minds Rise to Victory!**

Welcome to **Turn Court**, a dynamic debate competition where perspectives change instantly and only the most adaptable minds succeed.

In this event, participants will debate **trending and thought-provoking tech topics**. But there’s a twist — the moment the phrase **“Turn Court”** is announced, you must immediately **switch your stance** from positive to negative, or vice versa.

Here, **facts and opinions collide**, quick thinking is essential, and confidence is key.

---

## 📍 Event Overview

* Participants may register **individually or as a duo**
* If registering individually, participants will be **paired randomly**
* After **Round 1**, only **one participant from each team** will advance to **Round 2**

---

## 🔹 Round Structure

### Round 1: The Switch Debate

* Teams will be given a **debate topic**
* Participants must argue either **for or against** the topic
* When the phrase **“Turn Court”** is called, they must **immediately switch sides**
* This tests **adaptability, presence of mind, and depth of understanding**
* At the end of the round, **one member from each pair** will qualify for the next round based on performance

---

### Round 2: The Solo Showdown

* Selected participants will be given a **new motion**
* They must speak **both for and against the motion**
* Participants will **switch sides whenever instructed**
* This round evaluates:

  * **Clarity of thought**
  * **Confidence**
  * **Content strength**
  * **Spontaneity**

---

## 🧑‍⚖️ Judging Criteria

* Content and factual accuracy
* Confidence and clarity
* Ability to **switch perspectives smoothly**
* Overall presentation skills
