# Auction of Bytes

**Write the Code. But First, Buy It!**

Confused? Let us explain.

**Auction of Bytes** is a unique and exciting coding competition that tests not only your programming skills but also your **strategy and teamwork**. This event combines logical thinking, smart bidding, and competitive spirit to create a truly thrilling experience.

Unlike regular coding contests where questions are simply assigned, here **you must bid for the questions first**. Teams will participate in an auction to secure coding problems, and the **highest bidder wins the question**.

The competition is conducted in a **relay race format**, adding another layer of excitement. It’s not just about solving problems — it’s about **strategy, coordination, and speed**.

## 👥 Team Structure

* Each team must have **3–4 members**
* A total of **20 teams** will participate
* At least **2 members will compete in the coding round**
* The remaining members will act as **auctioneers**
* **Coders and auctioneers will be seated in separate rooms**
* **No communication will be allowed between them during the rounds**

---

## 🔹 Round 1: The Auction

* Each team will be given a fixed amount of **virtual funds**
* Teams must use these funds wisely to **bid on coding questions**
* Questions will range from **Easy, Medium, Hard to Extreme**
* Every team must purchase **4 questions in total**
* Teams that fail to secure **4 questions will be eliminated**

The questions acquired in this round will be used in **Round 2**.

---

## 🔹 Round 2: Relay Race

* The coding team will receive the **4 questions purchased in the auction**
* One member will start solving the **first question**
* After completing it, the next member moves on to the **next question**
* This continues in **relay format** until all questions are solved
* **No communication is allowed between team members during the relay**

The **top 3 teams** that correctly solve all questions in the **shortest time** will win.
