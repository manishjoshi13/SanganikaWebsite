# Design Dissect

**Where Precision Meets Design Thinking**

Welcome to **Design Dissect**, where precision meets design thinking.

In this unique **UI/UX challenge**, participants will analyze flawed digital experiences and redesign them into **clean, intuitive, and user-friendly interfaces**. It’s not just about making screens look better — it’s about **improving how they work**.

If you believe great design lies in **clarity, usability, and attention to detail**, this is your battleground.

---

## 📍 Event Overview

* Participants may register **individually or as a duo**
* The competition will consist of **two rounds**
* Participants will work using **Figma**
* Designs will be judged on:

  * **Usability**
  * **Visual clarity**
  * **Interaction quality**

---

## 🔹 Competition Format

### Round 1: The First Impression

Participants will receive **existing app screens with usability issues**. Your task is to **identify the problems and redesign the screens effectively**.

* Redesign the provided **broken or cluttered screens**
* Submit a **neatly organized Figma file**
* Submit an **interactive clickable prototype**
* Judged on:

  * usability improvement
  * visual craft
  * clarity

The **top performers** will advance to the **Final Round**.

---

### Round 2: Precision Under Pressure

Shortlisted participants will face **more complex redesign challenges** involving **multi-step flows and edge cases**.

* Advanced **multi-step user flows**
* **Prototype walkthrough** with annotated decisions
* **Accessibility considerations** will be evaluated
* **Creativity, depth, and interaction design** are key

The **best design under pressure** will be declared the winner.
